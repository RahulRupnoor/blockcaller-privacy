Privacy Policy – Call Blocker App    

🛡️ Privacy Policy for Call Blocker App

 

Effective Date: 30th September 2025

 

1. Information We Access and Process Locally

 

To function as a call blocking and screening utility, the Call Blocker App must access and process the following information **locally on your device**. This information is **never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services**.

  • Phone Numbers and Call Logs: We access incoming and outgoing phone numbers. This is required to decide whether a call should be blocked or allowed.

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any sensitive personal data, such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers.

 

2. How We Use Information

 

The information accessed (phone numbers, call logs, contacts list) is used **only** for the primary functions of the App:

  • Implementing your custom call blocking rules.

Since we do not collect any data off your device, there is no sharing or remote storage of your personal information.

 

3. Third-Party Services 

 

The Call Blocker App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No personal or call log data is shared with or collected by external parties.

 

4. Children’s Privacy

 

The app is safe for all ages as it does not target, market to, or collect any personal information from children under 13.

 

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

 

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here and updating the “Effective Date.” You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
